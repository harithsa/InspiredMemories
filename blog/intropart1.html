Introduction Part 1

Let's Code Friendship!!

People often tell me that there is no other thing is as beautiful as love.Love can be found everywhere from a boy towards a girl to the Mother's love. In between lovers love and parents love there is one such thing which made me better than them. Today I am going to talk about the love which adds the real spice to our life. Getting our partner in life will also adds spice but it is like a salt or pepper type. The real spice is what you get when you eat sweet corn in rainy days across streets and we use to eat Panipuri at roadside that tells you the real spice. The Value of that spice I am talking about is the friendship which keeps you awake all the time from text message to Meeting and from Quarreling to Sharing. I have seen many type of them in my life. This is just a platform for me to express what I have learn from them.

I want to take you all to every stage of friendship of my life and I try to relate them to this world. Everyday in this world people are busy in their life of surviving for fittest. Anyhow they work hard to be successful and work even smarter to rise everyday. There is a life where people are reserved and their emotions are never taken to the consideration by themselves. Companies provide the working freedom to their employees not to express the joy of feelings.

Before taking you to my friendship journey, Let me discuss with you about the world how it was and now. Present world consists of some cool stuffs to do out here. There are even infinite number of ways to bite some time. Let's consider all the professions in this world as a club dance in weekends, 90% of people thinks this profession is the only source to lead their lives comfortably. As the days goes on Needs are getting decreased by the wants of Greedy People.

I am no way different in that aspect concern. I too taught profession brings you Fame, Money & Everything you want. If you get what you want,Your life will complete and you will get the reason for your birth and it will be fulfilled. This is the most false thinking that I have every made. Friends, Family & People around me made me to feel like that. It is a Perception towards life which happened to be a great mistake. No people around me was thinking different than this. It seemed me that the world is all about knowing your wants and discovering the reason for your birth.
