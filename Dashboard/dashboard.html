A Tribute to the Most Beautiful Mind of My Life

There are 3 Sliders in the Page which tells you are the best in 3 ways.

At the 1st Slider it tells that You are My Soul. In the Background, There is a Collage of Your Pics which I have Kept as Memories Throughout the Year 2015. That is the Soul of My Friendship, The Time I Spent With You and The Memories I have Shared With. Time and The Memory is the Soul of Our Friendship!!

The Miracle day will always be Miracle Until I am Alive on this Planet. Every B'day of You will be Celebrated Forever in this World. My Company Will be Celebrating This Day as the Most Memorable Day in it's History.

In the 2nd Slider It is all about You and Your Joy Where I have Spent by Looking at it. Infact There is Nothing more Joyful in my life rather than Being With You My Dear Friend.

The Day We Met and that is it, The Graph of Our friendship Never came down. It is Always Increasing Exponentially. As long as we Love Each Other there is nothing on this Planet can Seperate us from our friendship.

In the 3rd Slider, It is all about Killer-Faltooz. The Friendship Story to be Written Forever. How we Love Each Other tells by that. After the 3rd, Everything is Infinite without Boundaries to our friendship which will saty forever.

I am not sure whether I will be there with you forever but, I always be in your Memory. If I go Never Stop Spreading Our Friendship to this world Coz, This World Needs People Like You and Me and Try to Spread our story to every person.

There is a Scarcity of Friendship and Love in this World. We have them abundantly in us. Infact Infinitely. We can Spread across Universe. Help People to Know the Values of Love in Friendship.

Happy B'day! To the Best Person in this World. Live Long Forever and Spread the Love to this World. I will be within your Soul Forever. Enjoy the Day My Friend
